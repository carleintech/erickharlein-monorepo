## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Consistency:** Try to have similar lighting/style across all photos
2. **Emotion:** Choose photos that convey the emotion of each chapter
3. **Quality over Quantity:** Better to have 2-3 great photos per chapter than many mediocre ones
4. **Storytelling:** Photos should complement the text, not repeat it
5. **Personal Touch:** Candid photos often work better than posed shots
